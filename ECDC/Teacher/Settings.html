Requirements


  • You must use a phone number that you own to ensure the centre can contact you at given moment.
  • You must have any call-blocking settings, apps or task killers disabled.
  • You must use your personal email for us to send news feed or update to the center straight to the email address supplied.
  • The documents submitted must be valid and submitted in order for you to see the programmes you have registered for.

Registration

  • The screen appears when you're creating a new account or signing in on the ECD home page.
  • When you have created your account/entered the system, you must register your child to the centre in order to enrol to the programmes. Without this, you will have very limited navigation options.
  • At this point, you should have successfully registered into the system and see your child/children information on your account. Should there be any further complication, contact the centre for assistance regarding the issue.
